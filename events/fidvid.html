About the Event


Prizes worth: ₹ COMING SOON/-


Entry Fee: FREE


EVENT DESCRIPTION

This is an online event. Participants have to fidget with any small object like fidget spinners, pen, yoyo, juggling balls or anything that can be fidgeted with their hands and make a video out of it.


Team Size: 1-3 members.


Rules and Regulations

  • Minimum length of the video: 15sec and maximum length of the video: 1 min
  • The object you choose to fidget with can’t be dropped throughout the duration of the video
  • Video should not contain any explicit content
  • The video should not be in HDR format
  • Decision of Sprout IT’17 will be final in case of any issues
  • The winner will be judged on the basis of creativity and fidgeting skills
  • The decision of the iTELF committee will be final and binding

Window of the event: 1st September, 2017 07:00 pm to 8th September, 2017 07:00 pm.
