About the Event

“If opportunity doesn’t knock, create a door”

Prizes worth: ₹ COMING SOON/-


Entry Fee: ₹ COMING SOON/- Per Team


EVENT DESCRIPTION

This event provides an opportunity to the participants to put themselves in the shoes of the marketing maniacs. So get ready to brainstorm innovative marketing strategies and test your negotiation skills.


Rules and Regulations

  • Team size – 4 members per team
  • Participants are requested to carry their respective college or office ID cards, which will be examined at the entry point
  • Registration receipt is mandatory without which no team can participate in the event
  • Participants have to adhere to the time lines provided. Teams that are not on time for the event will be disqualified
  • Participants are requested to perform the tasks with the props provided by the volunteers
  • Participants are strictly prohibited from entering into the out of bound areas of SIC
  • Participants are requested to portray their creativity in the event and are not allowed to inherit the ideas of the other teams. Any similarity of thoughts in the marketing strategy round will not be entertained
  • During the process of negotiation, the team that reports first to the volunteers will be given the first priority and it will be the volunteers’ call to decide on which team to address
  • It is essential for the teams to strictly follow the instructions provided by the volunteer and act accordingly, to win the event
  • At the end of every task, decision taken by the judges will be final and no objection will be entertained
  • Teams are expected to maintain the decorum throughout

Assessment Criteria

  • This event consists of two rounds of evaluation. The process of allocating the points for each round will be explained on the day of the event
  • Winners will be announced after the event. The decision will be final and binding
  • Judging criteria:
    1. Marketing Strategy
    2. Team work and coordination
    3. Communication and negotiation skills
    4. Creativity and innovation
